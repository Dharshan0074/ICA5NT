Guidelines

  • The Manuscript must be written entirely in English.
  • All the submitted research articles will be checked for plagiarism before the review process.
  • Plagiarism acceptance level of less than 20% and single source should be less than 5% will be considered for the review process.
  • To avoid plagiarism, proper and appropriate citation practices should be followed.
  • All submitted articles are subjected to a double-blind review process.
  • The authors are asked to submit the source file (MS .doc, .docx).
  • Author details should be included in the manuscript (Such as Name, Affiliation and Corresponding Author Emailid).
  • Please provide a 150-250 word abstract. There should be no undefined abbreviations or references in the abstract.
  • Please provide four to six keywords that can be used for indexing.
  • If there are any conflicts of interest/competing interests, they must be disclosed.
  • Funding information, Ethical Statement and acknowledgement to be included before the reference section.
  • References should follow APA format.
  • After review, you will receive a notification for Revision / Rejection / Acceptance.

Plagiarism Policy

  • Papers are reviewed on the basis that they do not contain plagiarized material and have not been submitted to any other conference at the same time (double submission).
  • Follow these links to learn more:
    • IEEE Policy on Plagiarism
    • IEEE Policy on Double Submission
  • All accepted papers and presented during the conference will be published in the conference proceedings and submitted for inclusion in IEEE Xplore Digital Library.
  • IEEE reserves the right to exclude a paper from distribution after the conference if the paper is not presented at the conference.

Plagiarism FAQ

  • Authors are insisted to submit their original work without plagiarism.
  • If plagiarism is found, authors will be responsible for any consequences.
  • Authors are responsible for rejection of manuscript based on plagiarism report.

Copyright Form

  • Transferring copyright is a necessary requirement for publication, except for material in the public domain or which is reprinted with permission from a previously published, copyrighted publication.

IEEE Cross Check

  • The ICACCS 2024 uses IEEE Cross Check plagiarism detection software to screen the submissions. If plagiarism is found, the IEEE guidelines on plagiarism will be followed.
